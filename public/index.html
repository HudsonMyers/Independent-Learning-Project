About Me

Hudson Myers

Student at Coastal Carolina University, pursuing a degree in Business Management with a focus in Entrepreneurship

My name is Hudson Myers, and as a student with ADHD, I often struggled to connect the daily curriculum with my long-term career goals. This project was born from that very challenge. I've created an interactive learning platform that uses AI to reframe academic subjects in a way that is personally relevant to you, helping you master core knowledge by connecting it directly to your own interests.

My Mission

To make learning meaningful and accessible by connecting core curriculum to a student's personal passions, empowering every learner to succeed on their own terms.
